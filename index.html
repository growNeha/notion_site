Product

I am trying to transition to product role and it has been a rollercoaster so far. At one point I realised sitting at home and reading product case studies and watching YouTube videos is a bad way to get good at product management. So I moved to Bangalore, signed up as a volunteer at HelloMeets and started hosting product meetups. This got me acquainted with people who are into product domain already. From there I got to know that taking part in product teardowns is a good way to practice product skills, and so far I’ve taken part in 2 teardowns where I got into top 5 in my second attempt. A lot of learning also came from interviews I didn’t clear. Here is my learning process, in order of recency, latest to oldest.
